# Merge Game

### Title
Pixel Mansion - Merge Mystery

### Plot Summary
Julia's tranquil drive takes a dramatic turn when she crashes near a mysterious pixelated mansion in Whispering Woods.
Despite her confusion, she feels compelled to explore its secrets.
To enter, she must use her merging abilities to clear the obstructed path.
Driven by curiosity, Julia embarks on a journey into the mansion's enigmatic world.

### Gameplay
A mobile merge game like Merge Mansion with worldbuilding like Homescapes/Gardenscapes
Uses pixel art style.
